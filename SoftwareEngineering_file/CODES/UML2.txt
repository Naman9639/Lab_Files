Library
a library lends books and magazines to members who are registered in the system also it handles the purchase of new titles for the library
popular titles are bought in multiple copies . old books and magazines newspapers are removed when they are out of date or in poor condition.
a member can reserve a book or magazine that is not currently available in library so that when it is returned or purchased by the library then the person is notified.
the library can easily create update and delete information about the titles, members, loan and reservation in the system
